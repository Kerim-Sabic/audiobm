# 07 Local Authority Operations

Manual business tasks. Do not buy links, create fake listings, create fake reviews, or manipulate review systems.

## Google Business Profile

- Claim or create profiles for Sarajevo, Banja Luka, Gradiška, Bijeljina, Doboj, Brčko and Tuzla.
- Use exact signage/NAP. Do not complete Sarajevo until address and phone are confirmed.
- Add branch URLs with UTM: `/poslovnice/[slug]?utm_source=google&utm_medium=gbp`.
- Add booking URL with UTM: `/zakazivanje?utm_source=google&utm_medium=gbp`.
- Use Hearing aid store as primary category where available; add Audiologist/Medical supply only if accurate.
- Add only services actually offered by that branch.
- Upload real photos: exterior, entrance, reception, audiometry room, team, products, accessibility/parking if true.
- Publish factual weekly posts: free hearing check, care tip, branch update, service reminder.

## Other Local Platforms

- Import verified GBP profiles into Bing Places.
- Set up Apple Business Connect where available.
- Check Waze, HERE and major local directories for identical NAP.
- Keep one NAP spreadsheet with exact names, phones, URLs and maps links.

## Reviews

- Ask only genuine customers after real visits.
- Use QR/SMS/email links per branch.
- Do not incentivize, script or fabricate reviews.
- Reply to every review within three business days.
- Escalate complaints internally before public response.

## Ethical Authority Building

- Local hearing-health education with senior associations and community groups.
- Partnerships with clinics/pharmacies only where ethical and factual.
- Local PR for verified branch openings and educational events.
- Educational articles with links to relevant branch/service pages.

## Monthly Reporting

- Search Console: queries, pages, indexing, sitemap errors.
- GBP: calls, direction requests, website clicks, reviews.
- Site: leads by branch, phone clicks, booking completions, online test completions.
- Operations: broken listings, stale hours, review response rate, new content opportunities.
